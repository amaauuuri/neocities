Profile: Amauri

Hello. I am Amauri, an independent developer, web designer, and synthesist. I build lightweight, responsive digital interfaces and explore the aesthetic intersections of vintage tech, Cybercore, and experimental sound synthesis.

My interest in computer tech started with digging into old operating systems, customizing terminal config files, and listening to low-fi synthesizer tapes. Since then, I've focused on keeping the web creative, craft-focused, and highly accessible.
